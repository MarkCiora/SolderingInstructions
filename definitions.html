Definitions

Soldering Preparation:Prepare wires and electrical components for soldering

Helping Hands: hold wires up for easier soldering

Wires: connecting piece of components

Wire strippers: takes coatings off the wire to expose the metal underneath

Flux: helps solder stick between two connecting wires

Shrink Wrap/Electrical Tape: cleans up the look of the soldered wires

Electrical Components: resistors, capacitors, microcontrollers, etc

Wire Cutters: snip the long legs off the ends of components

Soldering material:To solder this equipment needs used side by side (one in each hand)

Soldering Iron: heats the solder up for mounting wires/components together

Solder Wire/Roll: used in conjunction with the iron to seal together wires to circuits

Set around the workspace: Keeps the iron clean, the user safe, and help remedy mistakes

Brass Shavings/Wet Sponge: cleans any excess solder from the soldering iron tip

Fume Extractor: removes fumes from area and keeps user safe

Solder Sucker Gun: removes any excess solder
